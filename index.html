Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the HolyBite app (referred to as the “App”), created and offered by Apkerooo (referred to as the “Service Provider”) as a free service. This policy is here to explain how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

What Information We Collect

We collect a few types of information when you use HolyBite, including:

  • Your device’s IP address
  • Pages you visit within the App, how long you spend on them, and when you visit
  • Your mobile device’s operating system
  • Your approximate location (if you allow location access)

We do not collect precise GPS location data, and we do not track or store any personal health data unless you share it voluntarily within the app’s features.

How We Use Your Info

We use your information to:

  • Make the app work smoothly and efficiently
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Understand how people are using the App (using anonymous analytics)
  • Sometimes contact you with important updates or offers (only if you’ve given permission)

Third-Party Services

We may use a few trusted third-party tools to help us run the App better. These services may collect anonymous usage data:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

We never sell your data. We may only share information when:

  • Legally required to do so
  • Investigating fraud or abuse
  • Working with trusted service providers who follow strict privacy standards

Your Choices

You’re always in control. You can:

  • Disable location access from your phone settings
  • Uninstall the App to stop data collection
  • Email us to delete any data we may have stored about you

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed for the purposes mentioned above. If you’d like your data deleted, just email us at apkerooo@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we’ll delete it immediately. If you’re a parent or guardian and are aware of this happening, please reach out to us.

Keeping Your Info Safe

We care about your privacy. We use security measures (digital and physical) to protect your information. However, please remember no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.
